PRACTICAL 1

A paragraph is a self-contained unit of writing, usually 100–200 words (or 3–5 sentences), focused on developing a single idea, topic, or point. It generally consists of a topic sentence, supporting sentences with evidence or details, and a concluding sentence, serving as a building block for organizing essays and texts.
